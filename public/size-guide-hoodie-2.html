Discover our size guide to choose the perfect fit for you.
If you have any doubts, feel free to refer to our chart below!

A - Length

Place one end of the measuring tape at the collar (at the highest point of the shoulder).
Stretch the tape down to the bottom of the garment.

B - Chest

Measure around the widest part of your chest.
Keep the measuring tape horizontal.

C - Sleeve Length

Place one end of the measuring tape at the top of the raglan sleeve, then pull the tape down until you reach the wrist.
