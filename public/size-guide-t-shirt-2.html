Discover our size guide to choose the perfect fit for you.
If you have any doubts, feel free to refer to our chart below!

A - Length

Place one end of the measuring tape at the collar (at the highest point of the shoulder) and stretch the tape down to the bottom of the garment.

B - Chest

Measure the widest part of your chest.
Keep the measuring tape horizontal.

C - Sleeve Length

Place the end of the tape at the center of the back of the collar then pull it along the top seam of the sleeve When you reach the shoulder hold it in place at the shoulder and continue pulling down the sleeve until you reach the edge of the sleeve.
