Discover our size guide to choose the perfect fit for you.
If you have any doubts, feel free to refer to our chart below!

A - Length

Place the end of the measuring tape next to the collar at the top of the long-sleeve top (the highest point of the shoulder).
Pull the tape down to the bottom of the top.

B - Chest

Measure around the widest part of your chest.
Keep the measuring tape horizontal.

C - Sleeve Length

Place the end of the measuring tape at the center of the back of the collar, then pull it along the top seam of the sleeve.
When you reach the shoulder, hold it in place at the shoulder and continue pulling the tape along the sleeve until you reach the edge of the cuff.
