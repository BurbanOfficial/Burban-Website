Discover our size guide to choose the perfect fit for you.
If you have any doubts, feel free to refer to our chart below!

A - Chest

Place one end of the measuring tape on the fullest part of your chest and wrap it around (under your arms, across your shoulder blades) until you return to the starting point.



B - Waist

Place the measuring tape around the narrowest part of your waist and measure.



C - Hips

Place the end of the measuring tape on one hip and wrap it around the fullest part of your hips until you return to the starting point.
